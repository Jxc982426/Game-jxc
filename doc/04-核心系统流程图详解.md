# 04-暗黑挂机游戏 - 核心系统流程图详解

## 📋 文档概述

本文档使用 Mermaid 流程图和文字描述，详细说明游戏的各个核心系统的工作流程。包括战斗系统、挂机 AI、物品掉落、任务系统等关键模块。

---

## 一、游戏主循环流程

### 1.1 完整的游戏循环

```mermaid
graph TB
    Start[游戏启动] --> InitSystems[初始化各系统]
    InitSystems --> LoadGameData[加载游戏数据]
    LoadGameData --> CheckSave{有存档?}
    
    CheckSave -->|是 | LoadSave[读取存档数据]
    CheckSave -->|否 | CreateNewGame[创建新游戏]
    
    LoadSave --> SpawnPlayer[生成玩家到上次位置]
    CreateNewGame --> SpawnPlayer
    
    SpawnPlayer --> MainLoop{游戏主循环}
    
    MainLoop --> ProcessInput[处理玩家输入]
    ProcessInput --> UpdatePlayer[更新玩家状态]
    UpdatePlayer --> UpdateCamera[更新摄像机位置]
    UpdateCamera --> UpdateEnemies[更新敌人 AI]
    
    UpdateEnemies --> CollisionCheck[碰撞检测]
    CollisionCheck --> CombatCheck{发生战斗?}
    
    CombatCheck -->|玩家攻击 | PlayerAttack[玩家攻击判定]
    CombatCheck -->|敌人攻击 | EnemyAttack[敌人攻击判定]
    CombatCheck -->|无战斗 | SystemCheck[系统检查]
    
    PlayerAttack --> DamageCalc[伤害计算]
    EnemyAttack --> DamageCalc
    
    DamageCalc --> ApplyDamage[应用伤害]
    ApplyDamage --> CheckDeath{目标死亡?}
    
    CheckDeath -->|敌人死亡 | DropLoot[生成掉落]
    CheckDeath -->|玩家死亡 | RespawnPlayer[玩家复活]
    CheckDeath -->|都未死亡 | ContinueCombat[继续战斗]
    
    DropLoot --> GainExp[获得经验值]
    GainExp --> CheckLevelUp{升级?}
    
    CheckLevelUp -->|是 | LevelUp[升级处理]
    CheckLevelUp -->|否 | SystemCheck
    
    LevelUp --> StatUpdate[属性提升]
    StatUpdate --> SkillPoint[获得技能点]
    SkillPoint --> SystemCheck
    
    RespawnPlayer --> LoseGold[扣除金币惩罚]
    LoseGold --> SystemCheck
    
    ContinueCombat --> SystemCheck
    
    SystemCheck --> QuestUpdate[更新任务进度]
    QuestUpdate --> SaveCheck{需要存档?}
    
    SaveCheck -->|定时存档 | AutoSave[自动存档]
    SaveCheck -->|事件触发 | AutoSave
    SaveCheck -->|无需存档 | MainLoop
    
    AutoSave --> MainLoop
```

## 二、战斗系统详细流程

### 2.1 玩家攻击流程

```mermaid
graph TB
    AttackInput[玩家按下攻击键] --> GetSkill[获取当前技能]
    GetSkill --> CheckMana{法力足够?}
    
    CheckMana -->|不足 | NotEnoughMana[提示法力不足]
    CheckMana -->|足够 | CheckCooldown{CD 完毕?}
    
    CheckCooldown -->|未完成 | OnCooldown[显示 CD 提示]
    CheckCooldown -->|已完成 | CastSkill[释放技能]
    
    CastSkill --> PlayAnimation[播放攻击动画]
    PlayAnimation --> ConsumeMana[消耗法力值]
    ConsumeMana --> StartCooldown[开始 CD 计时]
    
    StartCooldown --> GetTarget[获取目标]
    GetTarget --> TargetType{目标类型?}
    
    TargetType -->|指向性 | AimTarget[瞄准目标]
    TargetType -->|非指向性 | GetDirection[获取面向方向]
    
    AimTarget --> CalculateHit[计算命中]
    GetDirection --> CalculateHit
    
    CalculateHit --> HitCheck{命中判定?}
    
    HitCheck -->|未命中 | ShowMiss[显示未命中]
    HitCheck -->|命中 | CalcDamage[伤害计算]
    
    CalcDamage --> CritCheck{暴击判定?}
    CritCheck -->|暴击 | ApplyCrit[应用暴击倍率]
    CritCheck -->|不暴击 | NormalDamage[普通伤害]
    
    ApplyCrit --> FinalDamage[计算最终伤害]
    NormalDamage --> FinalDamage
    
    FinalDamage --> DefenseReduce[护甲减免]
    DefenseReduce --> ResistanceReduce[抗性减免]
    ResistanceReduce --> DealDamage[造成伤害]
    
    DealDamage --> SpawnDamageNumber[弹出伤害数字]
    SpawnDamageNumber --> PlayHitEffect[播放受击特效]
    PlayHitEffect --> CheckDeath{目标生命<=0?}
    
    CheckDeath -->|是 | TriggerDeath[触发死亡事件]
    CheckDeath -->|否 | AttackEnd[攻击结束]
    
    TriggerDeath --> AddExp[添加经验]
    AddExp --> DropItems[掉落物品]
    DropItems --> AttackEnd
    
    ShowMiss --> AttackEnd
    OnCooldown --> AttackEnd
    NotEnoughMana --> AttackEnd
```

### 2.2 伤害计算子流程

```mermaid
graph TB
    StartCalc[开始伤害计算] --> GetAttacker[获取攻击方数据]
    GetAttacker --> GetDefender[获取防御方数据]
    
    GetAttacker --> BaseDamage[基础伤害 = 武器 DPS * 技能倍率]
    BaseDamage --> AddAttribute[+ 属性加成<br/>力量/智力/敏捷]
    AddAttribute --> AddBuff[+ BUFF 效果]
    AddBuff --> RandomVariance[* 随机浮动 0.9-1.1]
    
    RandomVariance --> PreCritDamage[暴击前伤害]
    
    PreCritDamage --> RollCrit{随机数 < 暴击率?}
    RollCrit -->|是 | CritMultiply[* 暴击倍率<br/>默认 1.5x]
    RollCrit -->|否 | NoCrit[不暴击]
    
    CritMultiply --> HitRoll{命中判定}
    NoCrit --> HitRoll
    
    HitRoll -->|命中 < 闪避 | DisplayMiss[显示闪避<br/>伤害=0]
    HitRoll -->|命中 >= 闪避 | ArmorCalc[护甲计算]
    
    ArmorCalc --> ArmorFormula["减伤% = 护甲 / (护甲 + K)<br/>K=1000*攻击方等级"]
    ArmorFormula --> ArmorReduce[物理减伤]
    ArmorReduce --> ElementResist[元素抗性减免]
    
    ElementResist --> PierceCalc[穿透计算]
    PierceCalc --> RealResistance[实际抗性 = 目标抗性 - 穿透]
    RealResistance --> ElementReduce[元素减伤]
    
    ElementReduce --> FinalCalc["最终伤害 = 基础 × (1-物减) × (1-元减)"]
    FinalCalc --> MinDamage{伤害 >= 最小值?}
    
    MinDamage -->|是 | ConfirmDamage[确认伤害]
    MinDamage -->|否 | SetMinDamage[设为最小伤害<br/>1 点]
    
    SetMinDamage --> ConfirmDamage
    ConfirmDamage --> ReturnDamage[返回伤害值]
    DisplayMiss --> ReturnZero[返回 0]
```

### 2.3 敌人 AI 决策流程

```mermaid
graph TB
    AIStart[AI 更新 Tick] --> GetCurrentState[获取当前状态]
    GetCurrentState --> StateSwitch{状态切换?}
    
    StateSwitch -->|切换到 Idle| EnterIdle[进入待机状态]
    StateSwitch -->|切换到 Chase| EnterChase[进入追击状态]
    StateSwitch -->|切换到 Attack| EnterAttack[进入攻击状态]
    StateSwitch -->|切换到 Return| EnterReturn[进入返回状态]
    StateSwitch -->|保持当前 | StayState[保持当前状态]
    
    EnterIdle --> PatrolLogic[巡逻逻辑]
    PatrolLogic --> ChoosePoint[选择下一个巡逻点]
    ChoosePoint --> MoveToPatrol[移动到巡逻点]
    MoveToPatrol --> ArriveCheck{到达?}
    ArriveCheck -->|是 | WaitTimer[等待定时器]
    ArriveCheck -->|否 | MoveToPatrol
    
    WaitTimer --> DetectPlayer{侦测到玩家?}
    DetectPlayer -->|是 | SwitchChase[切换到追击状态]
    DetectPlayer -->|否 | ChoosePoint
    
    EnterChase --> FindPlayer[获取玩家位置]
    FindPlayer --> CalcDistance[计算距离]
    CalcDistance --> InAttackRange{在攻击范围内?}
    
    InAttackRange -->|是 | SwitchAttack[切换到攻击状态]
    InAttackRange -->|否 | PathToPlayer[寻路到玩家]
    PathToPlayer --> MoveToPlayer[移向玩家]
    MoveToPlayer --> ObstacleCheck{有障碍?}
    
    ObstacleCheck -->|是 | RePath[重新规划路径]
    ObstacleCheck -->|否 | MoveToPlayer
    
    RePath --> MoveToPlayer
    
    EnterAttack --> SelectSkill[选择技能]
    SelectSkill --> CheckSkillRange{在技能射程内?}
    
    CheckSkillRange -->|否 | MoveCloser[向前移动]
    CheckSkillRange -->|是 | CheckSkillCD{技能 CD 完成?}
    
    CheckSkillCD -->|否 | BasicAttack[使用普通攻击]
    CheckSkillCD -->|是 | UseSkill[使用技能]
    
    UseSkill --> PlaySkillAnim[播放技能动画]
    PlaySkillAnim --> CreateProjectile[创建投射物/效果]
    CreateProjectile --> StartSkillCD[开始技能 CD]
    
    BasicAttack --> PlayAttackAnim[播放攻击动画]
    PlayAttackAnim --> DealDamage[造成伤害]
    
    StartSkillCD --> PlayerDeadCheck{玩家死亡或逃跑?}
    BasicAttack --> PlayerDeadCheck
    
    PlayerDeadCheck -->|是 | SwitchReturn[切换到返回状态]
    PlayerDeadCheck -->|否 | DetectPlayer
    
    EnterReturn --> ReturnToSpawn[返回出生点]
    ReturnToSpawn --> ArriveSpawn{到达出生点?}
    ArriveSpawn -->|是 | ResetHealth[重置生命值]
    ArriveSpawn -->|否 | ReturnToSpawn
    
    ResetHealth --> SwitchIdle[切换到待机状态]
    
    StayState --> CurrentStateLogic[执行当前状态逻辑]
    CurrentStateLogic --> DetectPlayer
```

## 三、挂机 AI 系统流程

### 3.1 挂机主循环

```mermaid
graph TB
    HangupEnable[挂机模式启动] --> InitHangup[初始化挂机参数]
    InitHangup --> LoadConfig[加载挂机配置]
    LoadConfig --> ScanArea[扫描周围区域]
    
    ScanArea --> FindEnemies[搜索范围内敌人]
    FindEnemies --> FilterTargets{有有效目标?}
    
    FilterTargets -->|无 | MoveToSpawn[移动到怪点]
    FilterTargets -->|有 | EvaluateTargets[评估目标优先级]
    
    EvaluateTargets --> SelectBestTarget[选择最佳目标]
    SelectBestTarget --> SetCurrentTarget[设置当前目标]
    
    SetCurrentTarget --> DistanceCheck{在攻击距离内?}
    DistanceCheck -->|否 | PathToTarget[寻路到目标]
    DistanceCheck -->|是 | CombatLogic[进入战斗逻辑]
    
    PathToTarget --> ObstacleAvoidance[障碍物躲避]
    ObstacleAvoidance --> ArriveCheck{到达攻击位置?}
    ArriveCheck -->|否 | PathToTarget
    ArriveCheck -->|是 | CombatLogic
    
    CombatLogic --> HealthCheck{自身血量安全?}
    HealthCheck -->|危险 | EmergencyPotion[紧急喝药]
    HealthCheck -->|安全 | ManaCheck{法力值足够?}
    
    ManaCheck -->|不足 | UseManaPotion[使用蓝药]
    ManaCheck -->|足够 | SkillPriority[技能优先级判断]
    
    UseManaPotion --> WaitManaCD[等待药水 CD]
    WaitManaCD --> FindEnemies
    
    SkillPriority --> HighestPrioritySkill[最高优先级技能]
    HighestPrioritySkill --> SkillAvailable{技能可用?}
    
    SkillAvailable -->|否 | NextSkill[检查下一个技能]
    SkillAvailable -->|是 | CastSkill[释放技能]
    
    NextSkill --> SkillAvailable
    
    CastSkill --> TargetDeadCheck{目标死亡?}
    CastSkill --> LootDropCheck{有掉落?}
    
    TargetDeadCheck -->|是 | LootLogic[拾取逻辑]
    TargetDeadCheck -->|否 | HealthCheck
    
    LootDropCheck -->|有 | LootLogic
    LootDropCheck -->|无 | FindEnemies
    
    LootLogic --> QualityFilter{品质过滤检查}
    QualityFilter -->|符合 | PickUp[拾取到背包]
    QualityFilter -->|不符合 | IgnoreLoot[忽略]
    
    PickUp --> BagFullCheck{背包已满?}
    BagFullCheck -->|是 | NotifyUser[通知用户]
    BagFullCheck -->|否 | FindEnemies
    
    IgnoreLoot --> FindEnemies
    
    EmergencyPotion --> PotionCDCheck{药水 CD 中?}
    PotionCDCheck -->|是 | Retreat[撤退]
    PotionCDCheck -->|否 | DrinkPotion[喝药]
    
    DrinkPotion --> RecoverHealth[恢复生命值]
    RecoverHealth --> FindEnemies
    
    Retreat --> SafePoint[安全点]
    SafePoint --> WaitRecover[等待恢复]
    WaitRecover --> HealthSafe{血量安全?}
    HealthSafe -->|是 | FindEnemies
    HealthSafe -->|否 | WaitRecover
    
    MoveToSpawn --> GetNearestSpawn[获取最近怪点]
    GetNearestSpawn --> Navigate[导航到怪点]
    Navigate --> FindEnemies
    
    NotifyUser --> HangupStop{停止挂机?}
    NotifyUser --> ContinueHangup[继续挂机]
    
    HangupStop --> DisableHangup[关闭挂机]
    ContinueHangup --> FindEnemies
```

### 3.2 技能释放优先级算法

```mermaid
graph TB
    StartSkill[技能释放决策] --> GetSkillList[获取技能列表]
    GetSkillList --> SortByPriority[按优先级排序]
    SortByPriority --> FirstSkill[取第一个技能]
    
    FirstSkill --> CheckEnabled{技能已启用?}
    CheckEnabled -->|否 | NextSkill1[下一个技能]
    CheckEnabled -->|是 | CheckUnlocked{技能已解锁?}
    
    CheckUnlocked -->|否 | NextSkill1
    CheckUnlocked -->|是 | CheckMana{法力足够?}
    
    CheckMana -->|否 | NextSkill1
    CheckMana -->|足够 | CheckCD{CD 完成?}
    
    CheckCD -->|否 | NextSkill1
    CheckCD -->|是 | CheckCondition{条件满足?}
    
    CheckCondition -->|否 | NextSkill1
    CheckCondition -->|是 | ExecuteSkill[执行技能]
    
    NextSkill1 --> HasNext{有下一个?}
    HasNext -->|是 | SecondSkill[取下一个技能]
    HasNext -->|否 | UseBasicAttack[使用普通攻击]
    
    SecondSkill --> CheckEnabled
    ExecuteSkill --> EndSkill[技能决策结束]
    UseBasicAttack --> EndSkill
    
    CheckCondition --> Conditions{具体条件检查}
    Conditions --> RangeCheck{距离条件}
    Conditions --> TargetCount{目标数量条件}
    Conditions --> BuffCheck{BUFF 条件}
    Conditions --> DEBUFFCheck{DEBUFF 条件}
    
    RangeCheck -->|满足 | AllPass[全部通过]
    TargetCount -->|满足 | AllPass
    BuffCheck -->|满足 | AllPass
    DEBUFFCheck -->|满足 | AllPass
    
    RangeCheck -->|不满足 | Fail[条件不满足]
    TargetCount -->|不满足 | Fail
    BuffCheck -->|不满足 | Fail
    DEBUFFCheck -->|不满足 | Fail
    
    AllPass --> ExecuteSkill
    Fail --> NextSkill1
```

### 3.3 拾取过滤规则

```mermaid
graph TB
    ItemDrop[物品掉落] --> ItemSpawn[生成物品实例]
    ItemSpawn --> StartDespawn[开始倒计时消失]
    StartDespawn --> PlayerNearby{玩家在附近?}
    
    PlayerNearby -->|否 | WaitPickup[等待拾取]
    PlayerNearby -->|是 | AutoPickupCheck{启用自动拾取?}
    
    AutoPickupCheck -->|否 | ShowPrompt[显示拾取提示]
    AutoPickupCheck -->|是 | FilterCheck[过滤规则检查]
    
    FilterCheck --> QualityCheck{品质检查}
    QualityCheck --> CommonCheck{捡普通?}
    QualityCheck --> MagicCheck{捡魔法?}
    QualityCheck --> RareCheck{捡稀有?}
    QualityCheck --> LegendCheck{捡传奇?}
    
    CommonCheck -->|是 | Pass[通过检查]
    CommonCheck -->|否 | NextQuality1[检查下一级]
    
    MagicCheck -->|是 | Pass
    MagicCheck -->|否 | NextQuality2[检查下一级]
    
    RareCheck -->|是 | Pass
    RareCheck -->|否 | NextQuality3[检查下一级]
    
    LegendCheck -->|是 | Pass
    LegendCheck -->|否 | SkipItem[跳过物品]
    
    NextQuality1 --> MagicCheck
    NextQuality2 --> RareCheck
    NextQuality3 --> LegendCheck
    
    Pass --> TypeCheck{类型检查}
    TypeCheck --> EquipmentCheck{装备?}
    TypeCheck --> PotionCheck{药水?}
    TypeCheck --> MaterialCheck{材料?}
    
    EquipmentCheck -->|启用 | PassType[通过类型检查]
    PotionCheck -->|启用 | PassType
    MaterialCheck -->|启用 | PassType
    
    EquipmentCheck -->|禁用 | NextType1[检查下一类型]
    PotionCheck -->|禁用 | NextType2[检查下一类型]
    MaterialCheck -->|禁用 | SkipItem
    
    NextType1 --> PotionCheck
    NextType2 --> MaterialCheck
    
    PassType --> ValueCheck{价值检查}
    ValueCheck --> MinValue{大于最小价值?}
    MinValue -->|是 | FinalPass[最终通过]
    MinValue -->|否 | SkipItem
    
    FinalPass --> MoveToItem[移动到物品]
    MoveToItem --> PickupRange{进入拾取范围?}
    PickupRange -->|否 | MoveToItem
    PickupRange -->|是 | AddToBag[添加到背包]
    
    AddToItem --> BagCheck{背包容量检查}
    BagCheck -->|满 | CannotPickup[无法拾取]
    BagCheck -->|不满 | SuccessPickup[拾取成功]
    
    SuccessPickup --> ShowNotification[显示拾取通知]
    ShowNotification --> FindEnemies[返回战斗]
    
    CannotPickup --> AlertUser[警告用户背包已满]
    AlertUser --> FindEnemies
    
    ShowPrompt --> ManualPickup{手动拾取?}
    ManualPickup -->|是 | AddToBag
    ManualPickup -->|否 | SkipItem
    
    SkipItem --> WaitDespawn[等待消失]
    WaitDespawn --> DespawnTime{时间到?}
    DespawnTime -->|是 | RemoveItem[移除物品]
    DespawnTime -->|否 | PlayerNearby
    
    RemoveItem --> EndFlow[流程结束]
    FindEnemies --> EndFlow
```

## 四、物品掉落与生成流程

### 4.1 怪物死亡掉落流程

```mermaid
graph TB
    MonsterDie[怪物死亡] --> TriggerDeath[触发死亡事件]
    TriggerDeath --> LoadLootTable[加载掉落表]
    LoadLootTable --> GetDropEntries[获取掉落条目]
    
    GetDropEntries --> FirstEntry[处理第一条目]
    FirstEntry --> RollChance{随机掉落判定}
    
    RollChance -->|失败 | NoDrop[此物品不掉落]
    RollChance -->|成功 | DetermineDrop[确定掉落]
    
    DetermineDrop --> GetItemID[获取物品 ID]
    GetItemID --> RollQuality{随机品质}
    
    RollQuality --> CommonRoll{普通品质}
    RollQuality --> MagicRoll{魔法品质}
    RollQuality --> RareRoll{稀有品质}
    RollQuality --> LegendRoll{传奇品质}
    
    CommonRoll --> SetCommon[设置为普通]
    MagicRoll --> SetMagic[设置为魔法]
    RareRoll --> SetRare[设置为稀有]
    LegendRoll --> SetLegend[设置为传奇]
    
    SetCommon --> GenerateAffixes[生成词缀]
    SetMagic --> GenerateAffixes
    SetRare --> GenerateAffixes
    SetLegend --> GenerateAffixes
    
    GenerateAffixes --> PrefixCheck{有前缀?}
    PrefixCheck -->|是 | AddPrefix[添加前缀]
    PrefixCheck -->|否 | SuffixCheck{有后缀?}
    
    SuffixCheck -->|是 | AddSuffix[添加后缀]
    SuffixCheck -->|否 | CalcStats[计算属性]
    
    AddPrefix --> SuffixCheck
    AddSuffix --> CalcStats
    
    CalcStats --> GetILevel[获取物品等级]
    GetILevel --> StatRanges[确定属性区间]
    StatRanges --> RandomizeStats[随机化属性值]
    RandomizeStats --> FinalItem[生成最终物品]
    
    FinalItem --> CreateInstance[创建物品实例]
    CreateInstance --> SpawnEffect[生成光柱特效]
    
    SpawnEffect --> QualityEffect{根据品质}
    QualityEffect --> CommonEffect[白色光柱]
    QualityEffect --> MagicEffect[蓝色光柱]
    QualityEffect --> RareEffect[黄色光柱]
    QualityEffect --> LegendEffect[橙色光柱]
    
    CommonEffect --> PlaceItem[放置物品到地面]
    MagicEffect --> PlaceItem
    RareEffect --> PlaceItem
    LegendEffect --> PlaceItem
    
    PlaceItem --> StartDespawnTimer[开始消失倒计时]
    StartDespawnTimer --> NotifyPlayers[通知附近玩家]
    
    NotifyPlayers --> ShowName[显示物品名称]
    ShowName --> WaitPickup[等待拾取]
    
    NoDrop --> NextEntry{有下一条目?}
    NextEntry -->|是 | FirstEntry
    NextEntry -->|否 | GoldDrop{金币掉落}
    
    GoldDrop -->|是 | CalcGold[计算金币数量]
    GoldDrop -->|否 | EndLoot[掉落结束]
    
    CalcGold --> SpawnGold[生成金币]
    SpawnGold --> EndLoot
    
    EndLoot --> GiveExp[给予经验值]
    GiveExp --> Complete[流程完成]
```

### 4.2 词缀生成算法

```mermaid
graph TB
    StartAffix[开始词缀生成] --> GetQuality[获取物品品质]
    GetQuality --> DetermineCount[确定词缀数量]
    
    DetermineCount --> CommonCount[普通: 0 条]
    DetermineCount --> MagicCount[魔法: 1-2 条]
    DetermineCount --> RareCount[稀有: 2-4 条]
    DetermineCount --> LegendCount[传奇: 5-7 条]
    
    CommonCount --> NoAffix[无词缀]
    MagicCount --> RollMagicCount{随机 1 或 2?}
    RareCount --> RollRareCount{随机 2-4}
    LegendCount --> RollLegendCount{随机 5-7}
    
    RollMagicCount --> AffixCount1[确定数量 N]
    RollRareCount --> AffixCount1
    RollLegendCount --> AffixCount1
    
    AffixCount1 --> FirstAffix[生成第一条词缀]
    FirstAffix --> AffixType{词缀类型?}
    
    AffixType --> Prefix[前缀类]
    AffixType --> Suffix[后缀类]
    
    Prefix --> GetPrefixPool[获取前缀池]
    GetPrefixPool --> FilterByILevel[按等级过滤]
    FilterByILevel --> WeightedRandom[加权随机选择]
    WeightedRandom --> SelectPrefix[选定前缀]
    
    Suffix --> GetSuffixPool[获取后缀池]
    GetSuffixPool --> FilterByILevel2[按等级过滤]
    FilterByILevel2 --> WeightedRandom2[加权随机选择]
    WeightedRandom2 --> SelectSuffix[选定后缀]
    
    SelectPrefix --> GetValue[获取数值区间]

    GetValue --> RollValue[随机数值]

    CountCheck -->|否 | NextAffix[生成下一条]

    NextAffix --> AffixType
    
    SpecialCheck -->|是 | AddSpecial[添加传奇特效]
    SpecialCheck -->|否 | Finalize[完成生成]
    
    AddSpecial --> Finalize
    
    Finalize --> ValidateAffixes[验证词缀合法性]
    ValidateAffixes --> RemoveConflict[移除冲突词缀]

    NoAffix --> CompleteAffix
```
